### Workflow (as taught by Justin)
1. Navigate to SM8 Dispatch Board: `https://go.servicem8.com/job_dispatch`
2. Search for job number in "Job Search..." textbox
3. Double-click job from search results
4. In job diary: Click **"Generate PDF"** link (creates Form Response + PDF)
5. Close PDF preview modal (Escape or close button)
6. Click **Billing** → **Send Quote** → **Email Templates** → **"New Service Contract Quote"**
7. Click first `.AttachmentIcon` (attaches Smart PDF)
8. Add CC: `696d5a@inbox.servicem8.com`
9. Click **Send**
