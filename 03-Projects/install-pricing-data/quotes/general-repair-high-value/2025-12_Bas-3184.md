## Description
REPAIR - Supply and fit on a Cibes lift door opener, transformer and Rectifier
Description

Scope of Works - Replacement of Door Opener, Transformer & Rectifier
Equipment:
Door Opener CAN-BUS VIT
Transformer
Rectifier
1.	Pre-Works Preparation
Attend site during normal working hours.
Carry out full isolation of the lift in accordance with current safety procedures.
Display appropriate signage and secure the machine room/landing area.
Inspect existing door operator assembly, transformer, and rectifier to confirm compatibility with replacement components supplied.
2.	Removal of Existing Components
Remove the existing door opener unit from the car top assembly.
Disconnect and safely remove the existing transformer and rectifier units.
Inspect all cabling, connectors, mounting points, and CAN-BUS wiring for damage or heat issues.
Clean the mounting area to ensure a safe installation surface.
3.	Installation of New Equipment
Install the new Door Opener CAN-BUS VIT operator to manufacturer specifications.
Fit and wire the new 230/19/24 VAC transformer.
Install and connect the new rectifier unit.
Ensure all wiring routes follow original manufacturer standards and are mechanically protected. 
4.	Commissioning & Testing
Power up the lift and perform CAN-BUS communication checks.
Carry out door speed, torque, and travel adjustments for reliable operation.
Test door opening/closing cycles on all floors.
Run the lift through multiple full travel cycles to ensure safe, consistent door operation.
Check for operational noise, vibration, or misalignment and correct if required.
Complete safety checks and confirm the lift is fit for service.
5.	Handover
Provide a full report of works completed.
Highlight any additional defects or advisories found during the installation.
Return the lift to service upon customer approval..
