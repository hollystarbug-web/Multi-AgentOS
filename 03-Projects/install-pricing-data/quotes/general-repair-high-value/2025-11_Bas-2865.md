## Work Done
Design, manufacture, supply, install, and test one bespoke 2000kg capacity goods lift, suitable for transporting trolleys, crates, and pallets across five floors.

Specification:
- Maximum load: 2000kg
- Rated speed: 0.15 m/s
- Drive type: Traction, with drive unit positioned at the top floor
- Power supply: 415V, three phase
- Total travel: 15,271mm
- Pit depth: 60mm
- Headroom: 2700mm
- Shaft dimensions: 1482mm (width) x 2980mm (depth)
- Cabin: Single entry, 1100mm (width) x 2860mm (depth) x 2000mm (height), powder coated steel walls, steel checker plate floor, interior LED lighting, and bump rails at two levels
- Doors: 5 x hinged landing doors (1100mm wide x 2000mm high), 1 x picket gate, car door in steel powder coated single hinged design
- Safety features: Buffer in pit, safety package for reduced headroom, protection with folding supports, inspection travel limit switch, monitoring of landing doors
- Lockable machine room door and partition included, constructed from painted perforated mild steel frames, bolted to floor and ceiling
- Roof steps made from aluminium tread plate included

Inclusions:
- One site readiness check (additional visits chargeable)
- Testing and commissioning of installed lift equipment
- Provision of O&M manuals (1 electronic, 1 hard copy) detailing operation, maintenance, decommissioning, and demolition procedures

Exclusions:
- Storage container
- Removal of existing equipment (by others)
- Landing hoardings
- Floor protection
- Provision of rotary isolator (by others)
