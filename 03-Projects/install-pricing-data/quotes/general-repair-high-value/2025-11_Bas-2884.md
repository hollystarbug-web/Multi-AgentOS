## Work Done
1) Remove and dispose of the existing platform lift and all associated controls from both the ground floor and basement.

2) Design, manufacture, supply, and install one new Hydraulic Step Lift.

Options included:
- Platform size: 1100mm wide x 1400mm long
- GSM autodialler on platform for emergency communication
- Centre opening door and gate
- Glazing to one side at the top landing, opposite the mast

Optional Extra:
- Powder coated in alternative RAL colour: £1,200.00

Technical Specification:
- Lift type: Hydraulic Platform Lift, type tested to Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC
- Shaft: Self-supporting, solid and glazed shaft panels
- Environment: Internal, stainless steel powder coated
- Capacity: 400kg (suitable for wheelchair passenger and attendant)
- Drive system: Electro-hydraulic
- Speed: 0.08m/s
- Travel: 1800mm
- Number of stops: 2
- Entrance arrangement: Open through configuration
- Power supply: 240V, 50Hz, 13A single phase
- Pit/ramp: 85mm recess at lower level
- Platform operation: Constant press (Up, Down, Alarm & Stop buttons)
- Landing operation: Single press, tactile buttons with coloured illuminated halo
- Shaft dimensions: 1320mm wide x 1550mm long
- Platform dimensions: 1100mm wide x 1400mm long
- Lower landing: 2000mm high centre opening door
- Upper landing: 1100mm high centre opening gate
- Voice annunciation: Announces direction and floor level on platform
- Digital indicators: On landing and platform
- Platform lighting: Maintained to illuminate floor and control buttons
- Flooring: Studded black rubber matting
- Emergency battery lowering: Platform will lower and door release in event of power failure
- Emergency manual lowering: Manual valve release on pump and manual door release
- Overload device: Audible warning and platform will not operate if overloaded
- Locks: Electric and mechanical interlock
- Standard colour options: Pearl White (BS22 B 15), Honey Beige (BS08 B 17), Goose Grey (BS00 A 05)
- Safety edge: Fitted to top of carriage and around platform to detect obstructions and stop travel
- Communication: GSM autodialler on platform
- Control box: Floor mounted, weatherproof enclosure (1150mm H x 800mm W x 350mm D), located within 5m of ram base
