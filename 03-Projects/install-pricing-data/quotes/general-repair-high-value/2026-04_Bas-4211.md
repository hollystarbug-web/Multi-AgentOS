## Description
We have a project in London, E8 2JS where top floor is being demolished.

There is an existing lift in the building, and the lift machine/mechanism is currently located on the top floor, which will be removed as part of work.

We therefore need advice and a quotation for either reduction of the lift shaft or removal of the lift mechanism, whichever is possible.

## Work Done
The existing unit is a traction drive lift and will require decommissioning prior to the top-floor demolition works.

Proposed scope includes safely lowering the lift car and counterweight onto the pit buffers, then cutting and removing the main suspension (lifting) and safety ropes as required to allow removal of the machine-room equipment located at top level. The remaining lift equipment within the lower floors and lift shaft will be left in situ for future review and/or full removal at a later date.

This quotation is dependent upon site access arrangements and the lift SWL (safe working load), and is offered subject to final survey.
