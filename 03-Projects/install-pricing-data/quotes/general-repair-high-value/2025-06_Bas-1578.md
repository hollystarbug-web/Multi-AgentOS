## Work Done
1) To rip out and remove the existing scissor lift from site and skip it away

&

2) To design, manufacture, supply, install and test one-off scissor goods lift (1000kg)


Technical Specifications:

Type: Goods
Carriage: Included via Hi-Ab
Capacity: 1000Kg EDL –Goods Only
Lift Stroke (LS): 1000mm
Raised height (CH + LS): 1220mm
Closed height (CH): 220mm
Platform length (L): 1680mm
Platform width (W): 1200mm
Lift time: Approx. 22 seconds to full height
Power: 3-phase 415v remote unit with 3m cable and hose.

Lift Furniture:
(for furniture positioning please refer to the diagram attached to the quotation)
• 1100mm high bi-parting interlocked gate to travel with the lift to side L2 to travel with the lift.
Mild steel construction made from 40 x 40mm box with mid-rail and kickplate
• 1100mm high bi-parting interlocked gate to stand at upper level offside L1.
Mild steel construction made from 40 x 40mm box with mid-rail and 1.5mm clad sheet for trapping protection
• 1100mm high handrail to side B2. Mild steel construction made from 40 x 40mm box with mid-rail and 1.5mm clad sheet for trapping protection
• 1100mm high handrail to side B1. Mild steel construction made from 40 x 40mm box with mid-rail and kickplate
• Treadplate platform for increased grip.

Miscellaneous extras:
• Galvanised mesh skirt to sides B2 and L2 for scissor protection
• Mechanical pit props for added rigidity when loading and off-loading at lower level
• Upgraded push buttons 1 x up, down, e-stop and 1 x up, down, e-stop and diagnosis LED for easy fault finding
• Lifting eyes to assist in positioning and installation of new Edmolift lift table these will be removed after installation
• Upper and lower level limit switches, when activated at the designated stops, the interlocked gates will release.
