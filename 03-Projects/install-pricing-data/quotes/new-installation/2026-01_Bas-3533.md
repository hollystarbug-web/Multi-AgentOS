## Description
quotation to either extend the existing lift (if feasible) or to supply a cost for a replacement lift?

The proposal/s would need to take into account the addition of two new floors and the requirement for the lift to meet the applicable fire rating standards for the new development, which will extend above 11 metres.

## Work Done
1) Remove and dispose of the existing lift from site.

2) Design, manufacture, supply, install, and test one Hydro Base passenger lift, FlexyLift R model, to serve seven stops (ground to sixth floor), with the following technical specification:

- Rated load: 630kg (8 persons)
- Drive system: Hydraulic, HAI 1:2 suspension
- Speed: 0.63 m/s (up and down)
- Travel: 16,000mm
- Shaft size: 1700mm (W) x 1950mm (D)
- Pit depth: 1180mm
- Headroom: 3900mm (clear), 3170mm (usable)
- Machine room: Below shaft, MRL cabinet position: Down
- Compliance: EN81-20, EN81-70:2021 + A1:2022 (accessibility), EN81-73:2020 (fire safety)

Cabin:
- Type: Purity S
- Internal size: 1100mm (W) x 1400mm (D) x 2070mm (H)
- Finish: Satin stainless steel (AISI 441)
- Floor: Smooth black rubber
- Handrail: K3, right side
- Mirror: Full width, half height, triplex glass with 4 brackets
- Ceiling: C40, mirror finish (AISI 430)
- Car operating panel (COP): Satin finish, B37Q rectangular red push buttons, GN-DOTL red arrow display, 24V, down collective operation, voice output (GMP3), alarm, overweight indicator, emergency communication (HELPY COMPACT V TRIPHONY), tactile and accessible features

Doors:
- Automatic telescopic doors (front entrance), 2-panel, left opening, 800mm (W) x 2000mm (H), satin stainless steel (AISI 441), VF7+ CANopen mechanism, E-120 fire resistance, standard aluminium sills, emergency evacuation (BCB 903011), CE lock
- Door frames: 120mm (L/R), 210mm (top), satin stainless steel

Safety and Control:
- Light curtain: Weco 03.G5.L174.230VAC+SUP 03.G5.PWS.FB4(US14)
- Unauthorised access prevention: Unlocking device kit for automatic landing door
- Controller: GENIUS 20, 9.5kW, 3~380V, simplex automation, absolute encoder, fire recall (EN81-73:2016), thermostat, relevelling, IoT connectivity (KLEEMANN LIVE with industrial SIM)
- Power unit: T350, 180 l/min, 9.5kW, 400V, EV100 1½" valve block, hand pump, max pressure 28.0 bar, relief 37.8 bar
- Ram: KZA, 110 x 7.5, two-piece, 8400mm length, RAL 5007 blue, rupture valve KL20 1¼"
- Emergency features: Typical hydraulic evacuation, battery backup, alarm, communication device, signals amplifier for hearing impaired

Landing Operating Panels (LOP):
- Satin finish, B37Q red push buttons, down collective operation, on-wall mounting, tactile and accessible features

All works are subject to final survey. Unless expressly detailed, the quote does not include any builders’ works or decoration.

The lift will be fully compliant with current accessibility and fire safety standards, and is designed to serve the new development including the two additional floors above 11 metres.
