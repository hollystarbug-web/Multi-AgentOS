## Work Done
1) Remove and dispose of the existing lift unit from site, including all associated components, carried out outside of normal working hours  with waste collection arranged during the same period.

2) Design, manufacture, supply, install, and test one Through Car Vertical Platform Lift, Base PublicLift Access model, with the following specification:

- Drive system: Stationary screw and rotating nut with automatic lubrication
- Capacity: 410kg (5 persons)
- Speed: 0.15 m/s
- Travel: 1300mm
- Number of stops: 2
- Configuration: Through car

Space Requirements:
- Floor aperture: 1505mm (W) x 1630mm (D)
- Pit depth: 50mm
- Headroom: 2240mm minimum

Lift Enclosure:
- Cladding: White vinyl-coated, double-skinned steel panels with EPS polystyrene infill, including partial cladding on the outside of the machine side at the top floor
- Lighting: LED ceiling lighting
- Fire rating: None

Lifting Platform:
- Platform size: 1100mm (W) x 1480mm (D)
- Console height: 1100mm to one long side, powder coated white (RAL 9016)
- Handrail: Grey, with LED down-lights
- Flooring: Armstrong Protech light grey vinyl
- Safety edge: Three sides of platform floor and top edge of console
- Controls: Illuminated with battery backup

Doors:
- 1 x GL fully glazed swing door (upper floor)
- 1 x EI60 swing fire door (ground floor, one hour fire rating)
- Clear opening: 900mm x 2000mm
- Door and frame finish: White powder coated (RAL 9016), anodic grey furniture
- Door operation: Manual with concealed self-closer, electrically and mechanically interlocked

Electrical & Power:
- Supply: 230V, 1ph, 20A, 50Hz
- Motor: 2.2kW, 400V, 3ph, 50Hz
- Controller voltage: 48V, 1ph, 50Hz
- Emergency lowering: Manual winding device
- Initiation: Soft start

Control Equipment:
- Microprocessor-based control with service memory
- Platform operation: Constant pressure button
- Landing operation: One-touch call button with green halo indication
- Face plate: Natural anodised aluminium with coloured text
- Push buttons: Flush and tactile
- Platform console: Destination, alarm, and emergency stop buttons
- Alarm: External audible sounder
- Pit prop: Manual with electrical interlock
- Fire alarm interface: Volt-free contacts
- Communication: GSM autodialler for two-way voice communication (EN81-41 compliant)

Compliance:
- Type tested and CE marked to Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC

Documentation:
- 1 copy of Operation & Maintenance manual

Included:
- 1100 x 1480mm platform
- Soft start
- Battery-operated emergency lowering from platform
- Ceiling and shaft enclosure with inbuilt LED lighting
- Arrival sounder

- GSM autodialler on platform (SIM card by others)
