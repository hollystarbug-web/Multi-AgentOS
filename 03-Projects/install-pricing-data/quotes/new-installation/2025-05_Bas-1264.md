2) Design, manufacture, supply, and install one new Chain Drive Platform Lift, fully compliant with all relevant British Standards and best practices. The new lift will provide travel from street level to the ground floor, with an approximate travel height of 1800mm, and is suitable for internal installation.

Options included:
- Platform size: 1120mm wide x 1480mm long

- Glazed landing door and gate
- Intercom on platform
- Fire drive facility
- Automatic shaft lighting
