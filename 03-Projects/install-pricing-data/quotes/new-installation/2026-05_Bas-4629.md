## Work Done
Design, manufacture, delivery, and installation of a bespoke Cabin Lift – Vertical Platform Lift to serve three stops. The lift will be constructed to the following technical specifications:

- Shaft and structure internal dimensions: 1550mm (W) x 1680mm (D)
- Cabin internal dimensions: 1100mm (W) x 1400mm (D), model C25-S (1.54 m²)
- Hydraulic unit: GMV HL 3010, 2-speed, 3H
- Load capacity: 400kg (up to 5 persons)
- Electrical board: Hats, 3 stops
- Stainless steel 304 skirtings, frames, and cabin panels (right, left, and back)
- Ceiling: Stainless steel 304 with 4 LED spotlights
- Flooring: S01 Black Altro
- Handrail: Model H02 Curve, stainless steel
- Mirror: Vertical
- Piston: 80x5/3.5 (supplied in 2 parts)
- Mechanics: Left (pre-assembled)
- Speed: 0.15 m/s
- GSM Auto dialler (SIM by others)
- Pit depth: 300mm
- Total travel: 6400mm (3,500mm between ground and first, 2,900mm between first and second)
- Headroom: 2,700mm

Doors:
- 2 x Automatic landing doors, CO 900, 2-panel, SS 441 FERMATOR
- 1 x Automatic car door, CO 900, 2-panel, 316SS FERMATOR
- 1 x Automatic landing door, CO 900, 2-panel, 316SS FERMATOR

Included options:
- 80/5 piston in 2 parts
- UPS rescue for cabin door
- PDF manuals
- Cabin photocell
- Hydraulic flat cable and oil (3 stops)
- Metal cabinet E300 (GMV)
- Shaft LED lighting for 3 stops
- Standard reinforced chassis with top angle brackets
- Cabin floor indicator TFT050 (5" colour, Hats)
- 3 x Landing indicators TFT025 (2.5" colour, Hats)
- 3 x Landing control panel logos
- English label stickers

Optional extras available on request.

Finishing works (optional, £2,200.00 per floor) include:
- Hoardings and barriers at each landing during shaft works
- Carpentry to box in frame and studwork, including fire rating to landing frame sides, headers, and walling to close and firestop all voids and gaps
- Ceilings and floors, including thresholds; plastering, beading, rubbing down, and painting to match existing RAL; screeding pit area to allow for finished floor level
