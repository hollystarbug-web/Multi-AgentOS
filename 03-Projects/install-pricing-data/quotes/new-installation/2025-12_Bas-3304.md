## Description
Scope of Works

Supply, delivery, installation, testing, commissioning, and handover of **one (1) new vertical accessibility platform lift (PLF1)** at **216 Oxford Street, London**, forming part of the 4th Floor & Mezzanine refurbishment.

The works include a **fully self-supported platform lift system**, complete with **contractor-designed and supplied steel shaft structure**, installed within an existing steel mezzanine structure (mezzanine alterations by others). The lift serves **three (3) stops**:

* Level 4 – Front Entry
* Level 4 Intermediate – Adjacent Entry
* Level 4 Mezzanine – Front Entry

Lift scope includes (but is not limited to):

* Minimum **400kg capacity**, wheelchair + attendant, Part M compliant platform
* Electric MRL or electro-hydraulic drive system
* Automatic power-operated swing doors with vision panels
* Open-protocol, non-proprietary control system
* Emergency lowering system, battery backup (ARD), manual winding facility
* GSM unit, two-way intercom, induction loop
* Shaft and pit steelwork, structural fixings, anchors, and brackets
* Platform, shaft, and emergency lighting
* Fire alarm interface and grounding/recall functionality
* Noise and vibration control, soft start, safety edges
* Fire-stopping coordination at shaft and structural interfaces

Testing, commissioning, and handover to include:

* Full load testing (weights provided by lift contractor)
* Commissioning by **NVQ Level 4 qualified engineer**
* Witness testing with project engineer (Ramboll)
* UKCA Declaration of Conformity and certification
* Provision of essential spares and three (3) sets of keys

Maintenance:

* **12-month comprehensive maintenance contract** commencing at Practical Completion
* Monthly service visits
* 24/7 call-out coverage with 2-hour response
* Entrapment response target: 45 minutes
