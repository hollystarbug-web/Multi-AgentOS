## Description
New platform lift
We are currently working on a private healthcare facility in Islington. The property is a three-storey building, and the proposal involves internal alterations to convert the existing teaching school into a healthcare facility, including the installation of a new lift.

As this is a private healthcare environment, our client is keen to explore lift solutions that are both cost-effective and visually appropriate. We would appreciate your advice on suitable lift options. Alternatively, could the interior of a standard platform lift be enhanced to achieve a more suitable aesthetic for a healthcare setting?

The floor-to-ceiling heights are as follows, and the floor thickness is 300 mm:
•	Ground Floor: 2400 mm
•	First Floor: 2400 mm
•	Second Floor: 2700 mm

## Work Done
1) Design, manufacture, supply, install, and test one-off commercial platform lift to serve 3 stops (Ground, First, Second) within a private healthcare facility, in accordance with the project scope and current regulations.

2) Supply and install lift with the following configuration and finishes (subject to final survey and coordinated drawings):

Capacity and performance:
- Rated load: 400kg (5 persons)
- Number of stops: 3
- Speed: 0.15m/s
- Travel: 5400mm total (0–1: 2700mm, 1–2: 2700mm)

Structural / space requirements:
- Internal shaft / structure (W x D): 1565mm x 1630mm
- Internal cabin (W x D): 1100mm x 1400mm
- Pit depth: 300mm
- Headroom: 2700mm

Drive and control:
- Hydraulic power unit: GMV HL 3010 (2-speed), 220V
- Piston: 80/5 in 2 parts
- Electrical board: HATS (3 stops)
- Emergency rescue: UPS rescue included
- Communication: GSM + audio autodialler included (4G SIM card not included)
- Indicators: Cabin floor indicator TFT050 (5” colour) and landing indicator TFT025 (2.5” colour)
- Landing direction arrows (Up/Down) included
- Shaft lighting: LED (3 stops) included

Cabin finishes:
- Cabin type: C25-S
- Wall panels: Brushed stainless steel 304 (left, right and back panels)
- Operating panel: Large brushed stainless steel 304
- Skirtings/frames: Stainless steel 304
- Handrail: Model H01 stainless steel
- Mirror: Vertical
- Ceiling: Standard black ceiling with 4 LED spotlights
- Flooring: S01 black Altro

Doors (manual swing doors as standard):
- Swing door: CO-900/120, RAL 7032, with vision panel (FERM)
- Swing door option: CO 900 S/D stainless steel 441 solid door

Included items:
- Export packaging
- PDF manuals
- Metal cabinet E300 (GMV)
- Fixings/screws
- 3 x landing control panels (with logo)
- English label stickers

Optional extras:
- Automatic telescopic doors (landing and cabin): £1,250.00
  Note: Changing to automatic telescopic doors requires cabin size reduction to 1100mm x 1330mm (W x D).
- Finishing works (making good around the entrances): £2,100.00 per floor
