## Description

## Work Done
GreenLight Engineering - Hydraulic Passenger Lift Refurbishment

Remove existing lift controls and drive system and upgrade to improve the service of the lift shaft, car and
machine room, upgrading the facilities to satisfy all health and safety compliances.
1) Remove all obsolete and worn shaft, car, pit and machine room wiring – ripping out car and shaft limit
switching, shaft positioning equipment, car top control unit, car operating panel, landing pushes indicators,
and oil for recycling. Rip out the old controller, tank and motor/valve wiring back to the mains isolator.
Dispose of and recycle.
2) Supply and fit a new fully collective controller and processor, digital Valve control including A3
unintended movement software, housed inside the existing controller enclosure.
3) Upgrade and/or install new shaft lighting as specified.
4) Supply and fit new mains SY cable and fitting to isolator and hydro pump motor, new motor, pump,
valves and tank, hydraulic piston, hydraulic rupture valve, hydraulic hoses and hoisting ropes as and where
specified.
5) Supply and fit complete new multi- strand shaft wiring including (CAT 5) where necessary for pushes,
indicators, new travel limits, safety pit ring, levelling devices and emergency stop circuitry.
6) Supply, fit and wire in new multistrand trailing flexes and fittings to (cat 5) where necessary to all lift car
facilities.
7) Supply fit and wire in a new halfway terminals box to be housed on the lift car roof.
8) Supply and fit new terminal/reset button stable proximity switches with magnets and fit new shaft over
travel limit switching.
9) Supply and fit precision speed encoder shaft positioning system, sensor, switching and monitoring
brackets and fixings. Upgrade shaft lighting.
10) Supply and fit new flush-mounted landing operating panels complete with anti-tamper fixings and LED
type push buttons incorporating tactile finishes to all floors and contrasted. Keywitch barrels included to
isolate each floor as necessary.
11) Supply and fix LX new car operating panel with LED type push buttons controlled and incorporating
Tactile finishes numeration for each floor, alarm, door open, voice speech synthesiser, digital dot matrix
indicator, engraved with SWL and emergency BASELINE auto dialler instructions finished in brushed
stainless steel.
12) Supply and fit a new auto dialler system to connect to a designated and independent GSM telephone
line to be programmed to BASELINE 24-hour emergency response.
13) To supply and fit all signage to pit, shaft, car and motor room to include hand lowering emergency
instructions, machine room notices, voltage warning signs, rubber mat, emergency release keys.
14) A new door system - a power door operator to the specification as necessary.
15) Cabin upgrades - if a cabin upgrade is specified, the quotation includes cosmetic reline of cabin to
specified finishes.
16) Perform testing to BS part 10 and certificate including test weights.
17) Handover to Client - TESTING AND CERTIFICATION - LOLER inspections and certificates issued as per
the specification.
Duration of works 3-4 weeks.
We will require a deposit of 50% in advance and 50% upon completion.
