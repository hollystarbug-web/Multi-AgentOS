## Description

## Work Done
To design, manufacture, supply, install, and test one Base PublicLift Access (PLA) platform lift for the North London Heat & Power project, providing access between ground floor and level 1 (2 stops). We are offering our external grade guide rails, which is the best protection we can offer.

Principal Specification:
- Type: Base PublicLift Access
- Drive system: Stationary screw and rotating nut with automatic lubrication
- Capacity: 410kg (5 persons)
- Speed: 0.15 m/s
- Travel: 3740mm
- Number of stops: 2 (single entry)
- Pit depth: 50mm (option for ramp in lieu of pit)
- Headroom: 2240mm minimum

Space Requirements:
- Floor aperture: 1505mm (W) x 1630mm (D)
- Shaft: 1475mm (W) x 1600mm (D)
- Shaft construction: Cladding on 3 sides in white vinyl-coated, double-skinned steel panels with EPS polystyrene infill. Rear wall concrete, side walls masonry blockwork.

Lifting Platform:
- Platform size: 1100mm (W) x 1480mm (D)
- Console height: 1100mm to one long side, powder coated white (RAL 9016)
- Handrail: Grey, with LED down-lights
- Flooring: Armstrong Protech light grey vinyl
- Safety edge: Three sides of platform floor and top edge of console
- Controls: Illuminated with battery backup

Doors:
- 2 x GL fully glazed swing doors (manual operation with concealed self-closer)
- Clear opening: 900mm x 2000mm
- Door and frame finish: White powder coated (RAL 9016), anodic grey furniture
- Glass panel: 760mm x 1520mm
- Electrically and mechanically interlocked

Electrical & Power:
- Supply: 230V, 1ph, 20A, 50Hz
- Motor: 2.2kW, 400V, 3ph, 50Hz
- Controller voltage: 48V, 1ph, 50Hz
- Emergency lowering: Manual winding device
- Initiation: Soft start

Control Equipment:
- Microprocessor-based control with service memory
- Platform operation: Constant pressure button
- Landing operation: One-touch call button with green halo indication
- Face plate: Natural anodised aluminium with coloured text
- Push buttons: Flush and tactile
- Platform console: Destination, alarm, and emergency stop buttons
- Alarm: External audible sounder
- Pit prop: Manual with electrical interlock
- Fire alarm interface: Volt-free contacts
- Communication: GSM autodialler for two-way voice communication (EN81-41 compliant)

Compliance:
- Type tested and CE marked to Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC

Optional Extras:
- Ramp in lieu of pit: £155.00
- Automatic door opener: £722.00 per door
- Type EI60 1-hour fire rated door: £580.00 per door
- 736mm high glass shaft panel: £183.00 per panel
- Voice synthesiser with directional indication: £516.00
- Shaft or door powder coated to optional RAL colour: Price on application
- Cladding on outside of machine side (if not flush against wall): Price on application
- L-bracket for upper level threshold: £427.00

Included Features:
- 1100 x 1480mm platform
- Soft start
- Battery-operated emergency lowering from platform
- Ceiling and shaft enclosure with inbuilt LED lighting
- Chromated (rust-proof) external guide rails
- Arrival sounder
- Mi Tower for installation
- GSM autodialler on platform (SIM card required)
