## Work Done
1) Remove the existing lift from the site.

2) Design, manufacture, supply, install, and test a custom non-enclosed vertical platform lift.

Options:

- Platform size: 1100mm (w) x 1400mm (d) included.
- 240V single-phase electrics with soft start and stop included.
- Gates and platform side panels in clear laminated safety glass included.
- Platform frame and gate frames finished in powder-coated steel included.
- Manual opening single swing gates with interlock included.

Technical Specification:

1. GENERAL DATA
- Model: B3000 Non-Enclosed Vertical Platform Lift
- Rated Load: 385 kg
- Rated Speed: 0.06 m/s with soft start and stop
- Drive System: Screw and rotating nut driven by 1.5 kW AC motor
- Floors Served: 2
- Travel: up to 500 mm (finished floor to finished floor)
- Configuration: Adjacent entry
- Environment: Internal

2. SPACE REQUIREMENTS
- Floor Area: 1550mm (w) x 1550mm (d)
- Pit Depth: No pit required - ramp provided
- Headroom: 2000 mm minimum above top floor served

3. PLATFORM
- Platform Size: 1100mm (w) x 1400mm (d)
- Carriage: 1100mm high vertical fascia with control panel on one long side
- Finish: Powder-coated steel
- Side Panels: 1100mm high in clear laminated safety glass
- Control Panel: UP and DOWN buttons and EMERGENCY STOP button
- Operation: Hold to run controls
- Push Buttons: Tactile braille push buttons with LED illumination
- Overload Protection: Audible indication
- Lighting: Push buttons on the control panel are illuminated with battery backup
- Flooring: Aluminium chequer plate
- Safety Edge: Pressure-sensitive plate under the platform to stop downward movement if obstructed

4. MACHINERY COMPARTMENT
- Construction: Positioned to the right of the lift platform (when viewed from the lower level)
- Finish: Stainless steel

5. ENTRANCES
- Platform Gate: 900mm wide x 1100mm high with clear laminated safety glass
- Manual opening single swing gate with interlock
- Left-hand hinged (when viewed from landing)
- Upper Level Gate: 900mm wide x 1100mm high with clear laminated safety glass
- Manual opening single swing gate with interlock
- Left-hand hinged (when viewed from landing)
- Opening Size: 900 mm wide x 1100 mm high
- Operation: Hold to run controls
- Push Buttons: Tactile braille push buttons with LED illumination
- Remote Call Stations: x 1

6. ELECTRICAL DETAILS AND POWER REQUIREMENTS
- Power Supply: 240 V, 1 phase, 50Hz, 20A lockable isolator with soft start and stop
- Location: Permanent 20A rotary lockable isolator externally positioned at a minimum of 2100mm high within 500mm of the electrical compartment
- Motor: 1.5 kW
- Emergency Lowering: Emergency battery lowering

7. STANDARDS
- CE marked in accordance with Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC

8. HEALTH AND SAFETY
- We will submit our method statement and risk assessment in our standard approved format only. No allowance for submitting documents in any other contractor-specific format.

9. OTHER PROVISIONS
- Delivery: Delivery only included. Ensure safe, clear, and level access.
- Installation: Allow 2 days in your program for installation.
- Return Visits: Permanent power (by others) is required from the first day of installation. If only temporary power is available, an additional £700 charge will apply for a return visit to commission on permanent power.
- Off-site Storage: If the site is delayed, storage charges are £80 per week per lift.
- Commissioning: We will commission, handover, and train your staff in the use of the unit, including emergency functions at the time of installation.
- Associated Work: All builders and electrical requirements are detailed on our drawing for approval and will be required prior to commencement of installation on site to enable the lift to be installed and commissioned in one single visit.
