## Description
Supply and fit new Stair lift to replace an old Cama

## Work Done
1) Rip out, remove from site, and responsibly dispose of the existing Cama stair lift and all associated components.

2) Design, manufacture, supply, install, and test one one-off Slim curved-rail incline platform lift (indoor) to suit the existing staircase layout, with the following specification:

Technical Specification:
- Control of bars and platform: Automatic version
- Platform dimensions: 1000mm x 800mm
- Load capacity: 250kg
- Platform access: Lateral (opposite access)
- Ramps: LH standard / RH standard
- Hand of installation: Right-handed
- Total rail length: 3.9m
- 3rd rail for slope < 20°: 0m
- Traction type: Standard (GT 750)
- Traction group position: Straight
- Wiring set length: 2.5m (standard)
- Unit colour: Similar to RAL 7011
- Rail colour: RAL 9006
- Lower parking: 90°
- Upper parking: Arrival and upper parking on the last step
- Rail fixing: With feet (3.9m)
- Radio transmitter (automatic): Included (2 no.)
- Receiver for radio transmitter (automatic): Included (1 no.)

Optional Extras:
- Auxiliary foldable seat (wander lead suggested): €241.00
- Safety belt for auxiliary foldable seat: €51.00
- Wander lead for attendant control (cabled): €106.00
