## Description
New lift for Paragon Design (like for like)

## Work Done
1) Remove and dispose of the existing lift from site.

2) Design, manufacture, supply, install, and test one CIBES A5000 vertical platform lift, including all associated controls and safety features, to serve three landings.

Included Options:
- 12-month parts-only warranty (subject to regular lift servicing)
- Weight testing in accordance with the Machinery Directive (400kg capacity)
- One-touch call buttons at landing stations; constant pressure operation from the platform
- Ceiling and LED lighting on top of shaft enclosure with timer
- Alarm and emergency lighting on platform, both with battery backup
- Emergency battery lowering, controlled from the platform
- Emergency fire drive interface (provided by others)
- GSM autodialler for emergency communication (SIM provided by others)
- Landing arrival signal at all three landing doors
- Landing indicator and voice announcement on platform

Optional Extras:
- EI60 1-hour fire doors and doorframes at all three landings: £3,900
- External powered door operators at all three landings: £3,300
- Removal of the existing lift including entrance protection: £3,250
- Return visit for test and commissioning if permanent power is not available: £970

General Specification:
- Model: Cibes A5000
- Rated load: 400kg
- Maximum speed: 0.15 m/s
- Drive system: Screw-driven, hold-to-run control
- Number of landings: 3; Number of doors: 3
- Platform size: 1100mm x 1467mm; Shaft cut-out: 1500mm x 1630mm
- Travel height: up to 6,500mm; Total shaft height: 8,800mm
- Pit depth: 50mm
- Shaft panels: Steel; corner profiles and frame: aluminium
- Platform colour: RAL 9006 Silver; Door colour: RAL 9016 Traffic White
- Shaft panel colour: Factory White (near RAL 9016)
- Indoor installation

Electrical & Control Details:
- Power supply: 400V, 3-phase, 20A, 50Hz
- Motor: 2.2kW, 400V, 3-phase, 50Hz
- Starting current: 18A; Running current: 4.7A
- Controller voltage: 48V, 1-phase, 50Hz
- Lockable isolator required within 2 metres and in view of the electrical cabinet at the lower landing
- Microprocessor-based control system with service memory
- Platform operation: constant pressure button; Landings: one-touch call button with green halo indication
- Platform console: destination, alarm, and emergency stop buttons
- Communication: Intercom for two-way voice communication (EN81-41 compliant)
- Alarm: External audible sounder
- Pit prop: Manual with electrical interlock
- Fire alarm interface: Volt-free contacts

Compliance:
- Type tested and CE marked in accordance with the European Machinery Directive (2006/42/EC)

Unless expressly detailed, the quote does not include any builders’ works or decoration.
