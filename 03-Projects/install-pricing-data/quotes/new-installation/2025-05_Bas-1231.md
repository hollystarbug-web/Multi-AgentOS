## Work Done
1) Removal and disposal of the existing lift from site.

2) Design, manufacture, supply, and installation of a bespoke Homelift – Vertical Platform Lift suitable for up to 3 persons (250kg), serving 5 stops (4 floors plus basement) within the existing shaft (approx. 1300mm x 1200mm). The system is designed for accessibility and compliance with current regulations.

Technical Specifications:
- Stops: 5
- Shaft size: 1300mm (W) x 1200mm (D)
- Cabin size: 950mm (W) x 940mm (D)
- Load capacity: 385kg (5 persons)
- Hydraulic unit: GMV HL50 3010 2-speed
- Control panel: 5-stop electronic board
- Handrail: Model H02 Curve, stainless steel
- Mirror: Vertical
- Cabin: C25-S (1m²)
- Right panel: K01 White
- Ceiling: White with 4 LED spots
- Speed: 0.30 m/s
- Pit depth: 1000mm
- Headroom: 2700mm
- Total travel: 10,814mm
- GSM audio phone included for emergency communication

Options included:
- 5 landing control panels with logo
- Pit security stair
- Cabin LCD display (3.2" blue)
- Full documentation (PDF manuals)
- Hydraulic flat cable and oil (5 stops)
- Metal cabinet E300 (GMV)
- Shaft LED lighting (5 stops)
- All necessary fixings and screws

All steelwork in 304 grade stainless. Installation includes all mechanical and electrical works associated with the lift system.
