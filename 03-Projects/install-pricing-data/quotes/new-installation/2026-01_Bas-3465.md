Included Options:
- Platform size: 1120mm (W) x 1480mm (L)
- Capacity: 500kg / 6 persons
- Emergency battery lowering, activated from the platform
- Intercom on platform
- Fire drive facility
- Automatic shaft lighting
- Voice annunciation for floor indication and safety messages

Technical Specification:
- Compliance: Type tested and CE marked to Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC
- Shaft: Self-supporting modular steel panels, RAL 9010 (white), 40 gloss
- Maximum load: 500kg / 6 persons
- Drive system: Encapsulated guided chain drive
- Motor: 0.55kW synchronous speed
- Control system: Microprocessor
- Speed: 0.15m/s, with soft start and soft stop
- Platform operation: Single press on landings, constant press on platform
- Push buttons: Flush and tactile
- Console buttons: Destination, alarm, emergency
- Travel: 3325mm
- Number of stops: 2
- Entrance arrangement: Open front configuration
- Electrical emergency lowering: Included
- Door locks: Electric and mechanical interlock
- Installation space required: 1280mm x 1590mm
- Pit depth: 60mm recess at ground floor or ramp
- Headroom from top landing: 2230mm
- Power supply: 240V, 16A single phase
- Emergency lighting on platform: Included
- Shaft and console lighting: LED
- Alarm: External audible sounder
- Communication: Intercom on platform
- Safety edge: 3 sides of platform and top of console
- Flooring: Anti-slip grey vinyl
- Handrail: Stainless steel
- Pit prop: Manual with electric interlock

Documentation: Full operation and maintenance manual provided.
