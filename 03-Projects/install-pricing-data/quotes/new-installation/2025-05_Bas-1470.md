## Work Done
1) Remove and dispose of the existing lift from site, ensuring all components are recycled in accordance with environmental best practices.

2) Supply and install a one-off straight incline platform lift, designed for outdoor use. The lift will feature automatic control of bars and platform, with platform dimensions of 1250mm x 800mm and a load capacity of 250kg. The platform will provide lateral (opposite) access, with standard left-hand and right-hand ramps included. The installation will be right-handed, with a total rail length of 4.5 metres, lower parking at the straight section, and upper parking flush with the last riser. The lift will be fixed on self-supporting, adjustable feet.

Finishes:
- Platform colour: Similar to RAL 7011
- Rail colour: Silver anodised
- Fixing colours: RAL 9006

Additional features include two radio floor controls, a weatherproof cover for the outdoor unit, and a small cover for the board control. The customer is required to provide a dedicated power supply with a minimum conductor section of 1.5mm², protected by a 4A fuse.
