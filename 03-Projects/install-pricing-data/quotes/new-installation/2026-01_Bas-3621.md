## Work Done
1) Design, manufacture, supply, install, and test one bespoke hydraulic platform lift to serve three stops, with the following specification:

- Load capacity: 400kg (5 persons)
- Internal shaft size: 1530mm (W) x 1630mm (D)
- Internal cabin size: 1100mm (W) x 1400mm (D), model C25-S (1.54m²)
- Hydraulic unit: GMV HL 3010, 2-speed, 3-stop operation, 220V
- Travel: Total 5936mm (LG, GF, FF), pit depth 300mm, headroom 2510mm
- Speed: 0.15m/s
- Entrance: Single entrance, automatic telescopic doors (CO900/ S.S 441). Solid door both car and landing.
- Interior finish: K01 White panels, large 304 brushed stainless steel operating panel, S01 Black Altro flooring, vertical mirror, H02 Curve stainless steel handrail, standard black ceiling with 4 LED spotlights, steel 304 skirtings/frames
- Electrical: HATS 3-stop control board, built-in emergency phone (audio only), metal cabinet E300 (GMV), shaft LED lighting at all stops
- Safety: Cabin emergency stop, landing up/down arrows, landing and cabin TFT indicators (5" and 2.5" colour), English label stickers
- Additional: Export packaging, PDF manuals, all required fixings, 3 landing control panels with logo with landing indicators
