Technical Specifications (provisional – subject to final survey and confirmation of shaft measurements)

1) Lift type and performance
Supply of a new hydraulic passenger lift package, complete with new car, car frame, hydraulic equipment, doors and controller, designed to suit the existing shaft arrangement and new stop configuration.
Rated speed: 0.60 m/s
Travel: 16,000 mm
Pit depth: 1,200 mm
Headroom: 3,600 mm
Configuration: Double entrance (front and rear openings, as required)

2) Hydraulic equipment (OMAR)
Supply and installation of the complete hydraulic drive package, including:
Hydraulic pistons: CS 110 x 7.5 x 8,500 mm (2 pcs)
Hydraulic power unit: HDU210, 180 L tank, 20 HP / 14.7 kW, 3-phase (supplied without cabinet)
Pump oil collector (210/S–320/S)
Hydraulic oil: HV46 microfiltered (200 L)
Hydraulic hose: 6 m, DIN20022 2SN, 1-1/4” (Ø35) complete with associated fittings
Piston tool (for piston Ø60–Ø230)

3) Car frame, guides and fixings
Supply of a complete hydraulic car-frame assembly and associated safety/protection items, including pulley protection, guide starter with spring bumper, wedges, rope clamps/terminals, and all necessary calibrated guides, brackets, support angles and structural fixings (including M12 x 110 anchor fixings) for installation within the shaft.

4) Lift car / cabin (stainless steel)
Supply and installation of a new stainless-steel cabin, manufactured in standard 441 stainless steel, including:
Double entrance cabin arrangement
Curved stainless-steel polished handrail (approx. L=1170)
Car-to-frame fixing brackets
Silentblocks / anti-vibration mounts
Car roof rail and associated roof components
Levelling skid set

5) Doors and entrance equipment (SELCOM EVO-AUG)
Supply and installation of new automatic door equipment, including:
Car doors: SELCOM EVO-AUG, side opening, 2-panel, right-hand, 800 x 2000, VVVF, stainless steel 441 (Qty: 2)
Car door sills: 80 mm (Qty: 2)
Infrared light curtains: 12/24V DC, EN 81-20/50 compliant, NPN (Qty: 2)
Landing doors: SELCOM EVO-AUG, automatic, side opening, 2-panel, right-hand, 800 x 2000, stainless steel 441 with infill panel (Qty: 5)
Pit egress device to ground floor landing door (EN 81-20/50) (Qty: 1)
Landing operating panel cut-outs/arrangements for EVO-AUG doors (Qty: 5)

6) Controller, fixtures and communication
Supply and installation of a new hydraulic controller package (Carlos Silva), including pre-assembled equipment:
Hydraulic controller, 35A, 380V, configured for 5 stops and double entrance operation
Car display: TFT 5.6" (Qty: 1)
Landing displays: TFT 2.8" (main/ground display Qty: 1; additional landing displays Qty: 4)
GSM 4G emergency communication pack (telephone + intercom/triphony)
COP and LOPs in stainless steel, with blue pushbuttons, round style with Braille, suitable for 5 stops

7) Shaft lighting
LED shaft lighting provision, including:
LED shaft light preparation
LED strip shaft lights (Qty: 12)
