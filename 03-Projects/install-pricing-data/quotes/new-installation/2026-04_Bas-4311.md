## Work Done
Supply, deliver and install one TSL 1000W wheelchair steplift complete with upper and lower landing gates, configured for a maximum travel height of up to 1000mm. Lift to be provided with a remote isolate key fob system and supplied with a 12-month manufacturer’s parts warranty (subject to the manufacturer’s warranty terms and recommended servicing). The offered unit complies with BS6440:2011, as it sits in a non-enclosed or partially enclosed liftway.

Technical specification and key features:
- Platform size: 1100mm wide
- Rated load: 385kg (suitable for heavy wheelchairs)
- Maximum travel: 1000mm
- Safety: Automatic wheelchair arrestor mechanism
- Controls: Simple, user-friendly operating controls
- Emergency operation: Battery back-up to allow normal operation in the event of a power cut
- Installation: Practical and cost-effective alternative to a ramp, with quick installation typically achievable within one day (site conditions permitting)

Available options (if required):
- Steel or plexiglass side infills
- Remotely located call stations
- Stainless steel finish in lieu of black powder coat
