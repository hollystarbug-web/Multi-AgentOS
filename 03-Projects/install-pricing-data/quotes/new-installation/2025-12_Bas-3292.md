## Description

## Work Done
Design, manufacture, supply, and installation of a bespoke homelift (vertical platform lift) to improve accessibility at 21 Holland Villas Road. The lift will be delivered and installed with a bare cabin and prepared floor, ready for the application of finishes by others in accordance with the client’s requirements.
The client may select finishes from the factory’s standard range and must confirm their selection prior to issuing the Purchase Order.

Technical Specification:
- Number of stops: 5
- Internal shaft dimensions: 1700mm (W) x 1920mm (D)
- Internal structure: 1600mm (W) x 1820mm (D)
- Cabin size: 1100mm (W) x 1300mm (D), model C25-S (1.54m²)
- Load capacity: 400kg (up to 5 persons)
- Speed: 0.3m/s
- Total travel: 10,814mm (individual floor travels as specified)
- Pit depth: 350mm
- Headroom: 2700mm
- Flooring: Prepared by others (20mm)
- Power supply: 220V
- Hydraulic unit: GMV HL 3010, 2-speed, 3-phase
- Piston: 80x5/3.5 (3-stage)
- Control panel: Large 304 brushed stainless steel
- Operating panel: 5 landing control panels with logo
- Communication: GSM autodialler (supplied with 5m flat cable)
- Mirror: Vertical
- Handrail: Model H02 Curve, stainless steel
- Ceiling: Stainless steel 304 with 4 LED spotlights
- Shaft lighting: LED, 5 stops
- Panels: Galvanised sheet (left and right)
- Skirtings/frames: Steel 304
- Pit security stair included
- Cabin display: LCDAP 3.2" blue (Hats)
- Manuals: PDF format
- Hydraulic flat cable and oil (5 stops)
- Metal cabinet E300 (GMV)
- All necessary fixings and screws

Doors:
- Automatic landing and car doors, 900mm clear opening, 2-panel, RAL 7032 finish, FERM mechanism
  • 1 left-hand landing door
  • 1 left-hand car door
  • 2 right-hand landing doors
  • 1 right-hand car door

Options included:
