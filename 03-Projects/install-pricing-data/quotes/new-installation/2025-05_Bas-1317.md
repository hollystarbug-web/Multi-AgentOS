## Work Done
Design, manufacture, delivery, installation, and testing of three (3) scissor platform lifts to the SJWS-HLE-ZZ-ZZ-SP-Y-66-0003 specification. Each lift will be installed internally with a through-entry platform configuration, providing access across one short end at the bottom landing and the opposite short end at the top landing. 

Key technical details:
- Quantity: 3 lifts
- Number of stops: 2
- Maximum load: 500kg (BS6440 compliant; lift rated to 700kg)
- Travel: 890mm (up to 1000mm available)
- Platform size: 1400mm x ?900mm usable (900mm clear width through doors), 1600mm x 1250mm overall
- Pit size: 1630mm x 1280mm x 245mm deep
- Elevating speed: approx. 58mm/sec
- Power: 3-phase, 415V, IP54 electro-hydraulic unit (remote within 3m)
- Construction: Mild steel base and door posts (powder coated RAL7024 or RAL9005), 304 stainless steel grab rails and faceplates, brushed finish
- Control: EN81-compliant, flush tactile buttons, white halo illuminated, IP54 rated, constant pressure
- Lift car: Frameless toughened glass enclosure, 1100mm high fixed barriers, power-operated single leaf doors (1100mm x 900mm clear), interlocked with lift operation
- Flooring: 25mm deep tray for inlay by others
- Mechanism: Hollow section scissor, chromed/honed hydraulic cylinders with hose burst valves, overload tested
- Scissor guards: Safe edge with black concertina bellows

Standard inclusions:
- Manual emergency lowering valve
- Hydraulic pressure relief valve
- Statutory warning signs
