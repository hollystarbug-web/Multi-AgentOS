## Description
Grosvenor Hill Court - Traction Passenger Lift Refurbishment

Quote to refurbish and bring two 8 floor traction passenger lifts back to compliance and reinstate.

Price will include modification to call and send buttons for operation from landings.

Works to be carried out are as follows;

A new lift controller, car operating panel, landing operating push stations, car top control unit, trailing flexes, flex hangers, halfway box, new wiring throughout machine room and back to the isolator, new shaft wiring throughout, replenish the main lifting gearbox, an autodialler programmed to service company.
Remove existing lift controls and drive system and upgrade to improve the service of the lift shaft, car and machine room, upgrading the facilities to satisfy all health and safely compliances.

As requested, to replace the obsolete control systems currently running the lifts and to fit a new British Variable Voltage Variable Frequency drive control system to provide consistent floor levels. Replacement of the GAL door operators to the latest version of MOVFR including the top tracks and suspension rollers. Replacement of the landing door top tracks door locks and suspension rollers.

1)Supply & fit new British VVVF control system and completely rewire the installation.
2) Supply and fit new 4.3” TFT landing indicators.
3) Supply and fit new landing push units including EN-Buzz
4) Supply and fit new car station panel incorporating 7” TFT Indicators, pushes, key switches, and emergency telephone.
5) Supply and fit a new four-way SafeLine emergency telephone.
6) Supply and fit a new halfway box.
7) Supply and fit a new car top control unit.
8) Supply and fit a new pit control unit.
9) Supply and fit a new Fireman’s control unit.
10) Supply and fit a rubber mat of sufficient rating to the motor room.
11) Remove all obsolete and worn shaft, car, pit and machine room wiring – ripping out car and shaft limit switching, shaft positioning equipment, car top control unit, car operating panel. Rip out the old controller. Dispose of and recycle.
12) Supply and fit a new fully collective controller and processor.
13) Supply and fit new mains SY cable and fitting to the isolator.
14) Supply and fit complete new multi-strand shaft wiring including (CAT 5) where necessary for pushes, indicators, new travel limits, safety pit ring, levelling devices and emergency stop circuitry.
15) Supply fit and wire in new multistrand trailing flexes and fittings to (cat 5) where necessary to all lift car facilities.
16) Supply and fit a new CT10 car top control unit with emergency stop and door operator controls.
17) Supply fit and wire in a new halfway terminals box to be housed on the lift car roof.
18) Supply and fit new terminal/reset button stable proximity switches with magnets and fit new shaft over travel limit switching.
19) Supply and fit precision tape head shaft positioning system, sensor, switching and monitoring brackets and fixings. Replace shaft lighting to LED shaft lighting
20) Supply and fit all new wiring and test to the main gear and lifting machine.
21) Supply and fit new flush-mounted landing operating panels complete with anti-tamper fixings and LED type incorporating tactile finishes to all floors and contrasted.
22) Supply and fix LX new car operating panel with LED type pushbuttons controlled and incorporating Tactile finishes numeration, alarm, door open, voice speech synthesiser, digital dot matrix indicator, engraved with SWL and emergency auto dialler instructions finished in brushed stainless steel.
23) Supply and fit a new auto dialler system to connect to a designated and independent BT telephone line to be programmed to service company's emergency response. OPTIONAL
24) To supply and fit all signage to pit, shaft, car and motor room to include hand lowering emergency instructions, machine room notices, voltage warning signs, rubber mat, emergency release keys.
25) Perform testing to British Standards Part 10 and certificate including test weights
26) Handover to Client.

The following are not included in this quote:
• Any building works.
• Any additional motor and gear modifications or improvements.
• Stoppages beyond our control and of your responsibility.
• The addition of weight to rebalance the lift.
• Any additional fire of security integration outside the lift motor room.

Additional information provided about the manufacturer:
• Current controller - Lift technique witter (?)
• New controller - Digital advance, zielberg break mec 32 Evacuation control in controller.
• SL6+ 4 line phone (original is wincrest?) 


*There is a 2 year warranty on all parts*

*All plants and components are supplied in the UK with stock in Bromley and Bradford, ensuring fast logistics of parts and eliminating the lift's downtime under planned maintenance going forward*
