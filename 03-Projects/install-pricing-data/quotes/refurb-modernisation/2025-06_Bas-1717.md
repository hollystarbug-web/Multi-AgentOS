# Bas-1717

**Date:** 2025-06-18 | **Status:** Quote | **Category:** Full Refurb/Modernisation

## Amount
**GBP 100,800.00**

## Address
Flats 1-20 New College Court Finchley Road Hampstead London NW3 5EX

## Billing Address
27 The Bentalls Centre Colchester Road Heybridge Maldon Essex CM9 4GD

## Description
2 lifts on site to refurb
1 lift flats 1-10
2 lift flats 11-20 
5 floors

Quote to refurbish and bring two 6 floor (G-5) traction passenger lifts back to compliance and reinstate - in the East Block & West Block.

Price will include modification to call and send buttons for operation from landings.

Works to be carried out are as follows;

A new lift controller, car operating panel, landing operating push stations, car top control unit, trailing flexes, flex hangers, halfway box, new wiring throughout machine room and back to the isolator, new shaft wiring throughout, replenish the main lifting gearbox, an autodialler programmed to service company.

Remove existing lift controls and drive system and upgrade to improve the service of the lift shaft, car and machine room, upgrading the facilities to satisfy all health and safely compliances.

1) Remove all obsolete and worn shaft, car, pit and machine room wiring – ripping out car and shaft limit switching, shaft positioning equipment, car top control unit, car operating panel. Rip out the old controller. Dispose of and recycle.
2) Supply and fit a new British made controller and processor. This will have a VF drive unit with long-life contactors & relays, with built in event and fault-logging facility.
3) Supply and fit new mains SY cable and fitting to the isolator.
4) Supply and fit complete new multi-strand shaft wiring including (CAT 5) where necessary for pushes, indicators, new travel limits, safety pit ring, levelling devices and emergency stop circuitry.
5) Supply, fit and wire in new multistrand trailing flexes and fittings to (cat 5) where necessary to all lift car facilities. These will be LSF (Low Smoke & Fume) to BSEN50214.
6) Install 3 new buttons conforming to EN81.
7) New shaft limit switches throughout. Fit all new shaft limit switches for position, slow down & stopping.
8) New car lighting with emergency lighting and diffusers where necessary. Replace shaft lighting to LED shaft lighting.
9) Supply and fit a new CT10 car top control unit with emergency stop and door operator controls.
10) Supply, fit and wire in a new halfway terminals box to be housed on the lift car roof.
11) Supply and fit new terminal/reset button stable proximity switches with magnets and fit new shaft over travel limit switching.
12) Supply and fit precision tape head shaft positioning system, sensor, switching and monitoring brackets and fixings.
13) Supply and fit all new wiring and test to the main gear and lifting machine.
14) Completely overhaul 1x p.d.o door gear.
15) Supply and fit new flush-mounted landing operating panels, complete with anti-tamper fixings and LED type incorporating tactile finishes to all floors and contrasted.
16) COP (car operating panel) will be of stainless steel finish, slim-line surface mounted with push buttons, autodialler, key switches & the engraving will include the load & logo. This will be with a car indicator - TFT indicator stainless steel face plate. Landing indicators TFT indicators surface mounted stainless steel.
17) Car and landing doors complete overhaul of rollers, door shoes & lock parts. New car & landing shoe liners.
18) Oil pots on car with drip trays fitted in the lift pit.
19) New ropes & termination supplied with rope certificate.
20) Paint the counterweight, the car steel work, pit & motor room floors.
21) Supply and fit LX new car operating panel with LED type pushbuttons controlled and incorporating tactile finishes numeration, alarm, door open, voice speech synthesiser, digital dot matrix indicator, engraved with SWL and emergency auto dialler instructions finished in brushed stainless steel.
22) Supply and fit a new auto dialler system to connect to a designated and independent BT telephone line to be programmed to service company's emergency response. 6 stored numbers conforming to EN81/70 and alarm filtering.
23) To supply and fit all signage to pit, shaft, car and motor room to include hand lowering emergency instructions, machine room notices, voltage warning signs, rubber mat, emergency release keys.
24) The car flooring will be of mondopave type as specified.
25) Perform testing to British Standards Part 10 and certificate including test weights.
26) Handover to Client.

This unit and the equipment we supply during this phase will have a 5 year warranty.
