# Bas-1841

**Date:** 2025-06-24 | **Status:** Work Order | **Category:** Full Refurb/Modernisation

## Amount
**GBP 26,190.00**

## Address
Ambassador House, 2 Cavendish Avenue Harrow HA1 3RW

## Billing Address
Odeon House 146 College Road Harrow HA1 1BH

## Description
Ambassador House lift refurb:

1) Remove all obsolete and worn shaft, car, pit and machine room wiring - ripping out car and shaft limit switching, shaft positioning equipment, car top control unit, car operating panel, landing pushes indicators, and oil for recycling. Rip out the old controller, tank and motor/valve wiring back to the mains isolator. Dispose of and recycle.
2) Supply and fit a new fully collective controller and processor, digital Valve control including A3 unintended movement software, housed inside the existing controller enclosure.
3) Upgrade and/or install new shaft lighting as specified.
4) Supply and fit new mains SY cable and fitting to isolator and hydro pump motor, new motor, pump, valves and tank, hydraulic rupture valve, hydraulic hoses and hoisting ropes as and where specified.
5) Supply and fit complete new multi- strand shaft wiring including (CAT 5) where necessary for pushes, indicators, new travel limits, safety pit ring, levelling devices and emergency stop circuitry.
6) Supply, fit and wire in new multistrand trailing flexes and fittings to (cat 5) where necessary to all lift car facilities.
7) Supply fit and wire in a new halfway terminals box to be housed on the lift car roof.
8) Supply and fit new terminal/reset button stable proximity switches with magnets and fit new shaft over travel limit switching.
9) Supply and fit precision speed encoder shaft positioning system, sensor, switching and monitoring brackets and fixings. Upgrade shaft lighting.
10) Supply and fit new flush-mounted landing operating panels complete with anti-tamper fixings and LED type push buttons incorporating tactile finishes to all floors and contrasted. Keywitch barrels included to isolate each floor as necessary.
11) Supply and fix LX new car operating panel with LED type push buttons controlled and incorporating Tactile finishes numeration for each floor, alarm, door open, voice speech synthesiser, digital dot matrix indicator, engraved with SWL and emergency BASELINE auto dialler instructions finished in brushed stainless steel.
12) Supply and fit a new auto dialler system to connect to a designated and independent GSM telephone line to be programmed to BASELINE 24-hour emergency response.
13) To supply and fit all signage to pit, shaft, car and motor room to include hand lowering emergency instructions, machine room notices, voltage warning signs, rubber mat, emergency release keys.
14) A new door system - a power door operator to the specification as necessary.
15) Supply and fit upgrade car lighting to type LED.
16) Re-seal the main lifting ram. Pressure-test hosing. Re-tension the main lifting ropes and certify.
17) Overhaul both sets of landing doors - fast and slow speed door panels, kicking rollers, track rollers and door shoes.
18) Supply and fit a new power automatic door open/close operator.
19) Supply and fit a rope data plate onto the carriage - main lifting ropes.
20) Perform a complete realignment of the lift carriage to include renewal/adjustment of guide shoes.
21) Reline the lift carriage internally to include the above teak covering. New LED carriage lights complete with emergency lights system and diffusers.
22) Perform testing to BS part 10 and certificate including test weights.
23) Handover to Client - TESTING AND CERTIFICATION - LOLER inspections and certificates issued as per the specification.

Duration of works: 3-4 weeks.

We will require a deposit of 25% in advance and 75% payment upon completion.

There is a 12 month warranty on all parts from time of commissioning.
