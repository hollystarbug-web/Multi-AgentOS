## Description
Refurbishment / moderation of Lift
Remove existing lift controls and drive system and upgrade to improve the service of the lift shaft, car and machine room, upgrading the facilities to satisfy all health and safely compliances.

1) Remove all obsolete and worn shaft, car, pit and machine room wiring – ripping out car and shaft limit switching, shaft positioning equipment, car top control unit, car operating panel, landing pushes indicators, and oil for recycling. Rip out the old controller, tank and motor/ valve wiring back to the mains isolator. Dispose of and recycle.
2) Supply and fit a new fully collective controller and processor, digital Valve control including A3 unintended movement software housed inside the existing controller enclosure.
3) Supply and fit new mains SY cable and fitting to isolator and hydro pump motor.
4) Supply and fit complete new multi- strand shaft wiring including (CAT 5) where necessary for pushes, indicators, new travel limits, safety pit ring, levelling devices and emergency stop circuitry.
5) Supply fit and wire in new multistrand trailing flexes and fittings to (cat 5) where necessary to all lift car facilities.
6) Supply fit and wire in a new halfway terminals box to be housed on the lift car roof.
7) Supply and fit new terminal/ reset button stable proximity switches with magnets and fit new shaft over travel limit switching.
8) Supply and fit precision speed encoder shaft positioning system, sensor, switching and monitoring brackets and fixings. Upgrade shaft lighting.
9) Supply and fit new flush- mounted landing operating panels complete with anti- tamper fixings and LED type push buttons incorporating tactile finishes to all floors and contrasted. Keywitch barrels included to isolate each floor as nesscary.
10) Supply and fix LX new car operating panel with LED type push buttons controlled and incorporating Tactile finishes numeration G and 1, alarm, door open, voice speech synthesiser, digital dot matrix indicator, engraved with SWL and emergency BASELINE auto dialler instructions finished in brushed stainless steel.
11) Supply and fit a new auto dialler system to connect to a designated and independent BT telephone line to be programmed to BASELINE 24- hour emergency response.
12) To supply and fit all signage to pit, shaft, car and motor room to include hand lowering emergency instructions, machine room notices, voltage warning signs, rubber mat, emergency release keys.
13) Perform testing to BS part 10 and certificate including test weights
14) Handover to Client - TESTING AND CERTIFICATION

Duration of works up to two weeks

We will require a deposit of 50% in advance and 50% upon completion
