## Description
Following our survey on 24/10/2025, we have recommended a full refurb of the lift unit.

## Work Done
Quote to refurbish and bring 6 floor traction passenger lift back to compliance and reinstate.

Price will include modification to call and send buttons for operation from landings.

Works to be carried out are as follows;

A new lift controller, car operating panel, landing operating push stations, car top control unit, trailing flexes, flex hangers, halfway box, new wiring throughout machine room and back to the isolator, new shaft wiring throughout, replenish the main lifting gearbox, an autodialler programmed to service company.

Remove existing lift controls and drive system and upgrade to improve the service of the lift shaft, car and machine room, upgrading the facilities to satisfy all health and safely compliances.

1) Remove all obsolete and worn shaft, car, pit and machine room wiring – ripping out car and shaft limit switching, shaft positioning equipment, car top control unit, car operating panel. Rip out the old controller. Dispose of and recycle.
2) Supply and fit a new fully collective controller and processor.
3) Supply and fit new mains SY cable and fitting to the isolator.
4) Supply and fit complete new multi-strand shaft wiring including (CAT 5) where necessary for pushes, indicators, new travel limits, safety pit ring, levelling devices and emergency stop circuitry.
5) Fully seal the main lifting gear.
6) Supply fit and wire in new multistrand trailing flexes and fittings to (cat 5) where necessary to all lift car facilities.
7) Supply and fit a new CT10 car top control unit with emergency stop and door operator controls.
8) Supply fit and wire in a new halfway terminals box to be housed on the lift car roof.
9) Supply and fit new terminal/reset button stable proximity switches with magnets and fit new shaft over travel limit switching.
10) Supply and fit precision tape head shaft positioning system, sensor, switching and monitoring brackets and fixings. Replace shaft lighting to LED shaft lighting
11) supply and fit all new wiring and test to the main gear and lifting machine.
12) Supply and fit new flush-mounted landing operating panels complete with anti-tamper fixings and LED type incorporating tactile finishes to all floors and contrasted.
13) Supply and fix LX new car operating panel with LED type pushbuttons controlled and incorporating Tactile finishes numeration G and 1, alarm, door open, voice speech synthesiser, digital dot matrix indicator, engraved with SWL and emergency auto dialler instructions finished in brushed stainless steel.
14) Supply and fit a new GSM auto dialler system with a 3-year multinetwork SIM to be programmed to service company's emergency response (OPTIONAL).
15) To supply and fit all signage to pit, shaft, car and motor room to include hand lowering emergency instructions, machine room notices, voltage warning signs, rubber mat, emergency release keys.
16) Perform testing to British Standards Part 10 and certificate including test weights.
17) Handover to Client.

*All plants and components are supplied in the UK with stock in Bromley and Bradford, ensuring fast logistics of parts and eliminating the lift's downtime under planned maintenance going forward*

*Price subject to final survey*
