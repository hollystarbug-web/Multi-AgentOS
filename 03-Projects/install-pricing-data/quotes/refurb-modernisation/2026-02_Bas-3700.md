## Work Done
Refurbishment of 2 stop Hydraulic Passenger Lift

Remove existing lift controls and drive system and upgrade to improve the service of the lift shaft, car and machine room, upgrading the facilities to satisfy all health and safely compliances.

1) Remove all obsolete and worn shaft, car, pit and machine room wiring – ripping out car and shaft limit switching, shaft positioning equipment, car top control unit, car operating panel, landing pushes indicators, and oil for recycling. Rip out the old controller, overhaul the tank and motor/valve wiring back to the mains isolator. Dispose of and recycle. Overhaul and seal the RAM and valve block.
2) Supply and fit a new fully collective controller and processor, digital Valve control including A3 unintended movement software housed inside the existing controller enclosure.
3) Supply and fit new mains SY cable and fitting to isolator and hydro pump motor.
4) Supply and fit complete new multi-strand shaft wiring including (CAT 5) where necessary for pushes, indicators, new travel limits, safety pit ring, levelling devices and emergency stop circuitry.
5) Supply fit and wire in new multi-strand trailing flexes and fittings to (cat 5) where necessary to all lift car facilities.
6) Supply fit and wire in a new halfway terminals box to be housed on the lift car roof.
7) Supply & fit a new door gear system.
8) Supply and fit new terminal/ reset button stable proximity switches with magnets and fit new shaft over travel limit switching.
9) Supply and fit precision speed encoder shaft positioning system, sensor, switching and monitoring brackets and fixings. Upgrade shaft lighting.
10) Supply and fit new flush-mounted landing operating panels complete with anti- tamper fixings and LED type push buttons incorporating tactile finishes to all floors and contrasted. Keyswitch barrels included to isolate each floor as necessary.
11) Supply and fix LX new car operating panel with LED type push buttons controlled and incorporating Tactile finishes numeration G and 1, alarm, door open, voice speech synthesiser, digital dot matrix indicator, engraved with SWL and emergency BASELINE autodialler instructions finished in brushed stainless steel.
12) To supply and fit all signage to pit, shaft, car and motor room to include hand lowering emergency instructions, machine room notices, voltage warning signs, rubber mat, emergency release keys.
13) Perform testing to BS part 10 and certificate including test weights.
14) Handover to Client - TESTING AND CERTIFICATION.
